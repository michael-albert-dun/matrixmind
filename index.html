    Instructions

    Aim

    Find the hidden matrix of coloured stones. Each turn, fill every space on the board and press the arrow to score the guess.

    Placing Stones

    Select a space, then choose a colour from the tray.

    • Number Keys place colours directly.
    • Arrow Keys move the selected space.
    • Delete removes the selected stone.
    • Enter submits a complete guess.

    Scoring

    Every row and every column is scored separately, just like a line in Mastermind.

    • dark pip: correct colour in the correct position.
    • pale pip: correct colour in that row or column, but in the wrong position.

    Row scores appear beside rows. Column scores appear above and below columns. Empty scoring spaces mean no match.

    Example: 2 x 3, Four Colours

    Secret

    Guess

    Row 1 scores one dark pip for Red, plus two pale pips because Blue and Green are both present but swapped. Row 2 scores two dark pips for Yellow and Red, and no pale pips.

    Column 1 has two dark pips. Column 2 has one dark pip for Red and no pale pips. Column 3 has two pale pips because Blue and Green are both in the column, but both are in the wrong positions.
